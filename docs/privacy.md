# Privacy Policy

Your privacy and security are paramount.

## Code Privacy

Google **does not** use your private code, workspace data, or agent interactions to train our foundation models.

- All local code remains on your machine.
- Only the specific context necessary to complete your prompt is sent to the AI API.
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

## Analytics & Telemetry

Antigravity collects basic, anonymized usage telemetry to improve the product (e.g., feature usage frequency, crash reports).

You can opt out of telemetry at any time in `Settings > Privacy > Telemetry`.

## Third-Party Services

Antigravity may interact with third-party APIs (like OpenAI or Anthropic) if you explicitly configure it to use those models. In such cases, the privacy policies of those respective services apply to the data sent to them.

For advertising choices related to Google services, visit [Google Ad Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads) or [AboutAds.info](http://www.aboutads.info/choices/).
